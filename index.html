Junior Software Developer

These pages connected to backend services via the Wazuh API, allowing real-time updates to threat detection policies and security settings. I collaborated with the engineering team to ensure secure, reliable API integration and focused on improving the overall usability of the platform for system administrators and security analysts.

AvMC Army Game Studio

Lead Software Engineer

Huntsville, AL | Jun 2024 – Aug 2024

At the Army Game Studio, I led a development team in building a digital board game that teaches students about the U.S. government acquisition process through interactive, turn-based gameplay. Using Unity and C#, I implemented core mechanics such as card interactions, currency management, and strategic player turns, while also designing and testing intuitive UI/UX elements. I collaborated with Army subject-matter experts to ensure the game aligned with real-world training goals. Our project was selected for presentation at the National Contract Management Association (NCMA) World Congress in Seattle and the DCTC Scholar Showcase in Washington, D.C., where I demonstrated the game to government professionals and academic leaders.
